E-Ink Voice Agent - Connector Pinout Reference
============================================

ESP32-C6-ePaper-1.54 GPIO Mapping
--------------------------------

GPIO  Pin  Function              Connected To
----  ---  --------              ------------
18    37   I2C_SDA               TCA9554, PCF85063, SHTC3
 8    32   I2C_SCL               TCA9554, PCF85063, SHTC3
 5    39   EPAPER_MOSI           E-Paper (SPI)
 4    40   EPAPER_MISO           E-Paper (SPI)
 6    38   EPAPER_CLK            E-Paper (SPI)
 7    37   EPAPER_CS             E-Paper (SPI)
15    35   EPAPER_DC             E-Paper control
11    33   EPAPER_RST            E-Paper control
10    31   EPAPER_BUSY           E-Paper status
19    36   I2S_MCLK            INMP441, MAX98357A
21    34   I2S_BCLK            INMP441, MAX98357A
22    33   I2S_WS              INMP441, MAX98357A
20    32   I2S_DIN             INMP441 (mic data in)
23    31   I2S_DOUT            MAX98357A (speaker data out)
 3    30   SD_CS                 SD Card (shared SPI)
 9    28   BUTTON_BOOT           Tactile switch to GND
 2    25   BUTTON_PWR            Tactile switch to GND

I2C Expander (TCA9554 on 0x3F) Output Pins:
0: EPD_PWR      - e-Paper power enable
1: AUDIO_PWR    - Audio power enable
2: (NC)
3: AMP_ENABLE   - MAX98357A shutdown control
4: LED          - Status LED (EXIO_LED)
5: VBAT_PWR     - Battery monitor power
6: (NC)
7: (NC)

Audio Codec (ES8311 on I2C):
- Controlled via I2C (GPIO18/8)
- I2S signals from main ESP32
- MIC1L input enabled to ADC (0x2A = 0x7C)

Display (GDEY0154M09 - 1.54" e-Paper):
- SPI interface
- 200x200 pixel resolution
- 24mm x 24mm active area

External Components:
- INMP441: I2S MEMS microphone (no I2C, uses I2S only)
- MAX98357A: I2S Class-D amplifier (SD pin for shutdown)
- LiPo: 3.7V battery, 1000mAh+ with TP4056 charging
